Excited to share that I've joined the WISER Quantum and AI Program 2026! Week one has already been incredible. Here's a snapshot of what we covered:

## Week 1 Highlights

**🤝 Orientation with the WISER Team**
The WISER Team kicked things off by orienting all participants and setting the stage for this fifth annual Summer Program — six weeks of immersive training in quantum computing, artificial intelligence, and optimization. An amazing group of people making this experience possible!

**🔬 Dr. Scott Crowder — IBM**
Delivered a compelling keynote on the future of optimization: how hybrid workflows blending classical computing, AI, and quantum technology are tackling some of the world's most complex industrial problems.

**⚛️ Dr. Seongmin Kim — Oak Ridge National Laboratory**
Showed us how quantum computing, AI, and high-performance computing are converging to accelerate materials discovery — solving optimization problems that were simply out of reach with traditional methods.

**💻 Catalina Albornoz — Xanadu**
Introduced us to PennyLane and the paradigm of training classically and deploying quantum.
